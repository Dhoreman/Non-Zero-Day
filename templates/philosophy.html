Philosophy

The philosophy of "Non-Zero-Days" was inspired by a comment made by Ryan from Reddit.

Many of us have been there, laying awake in bed at night, feeling guilty about the things we failed to do that day.
When this becomes a regular thing it can cause us to experience stress and anxiety.
If you recognize this problem "Non-Zero-Days" might be of help to you.

A Non-Zero-Day is a day where you do at least one thing, no matter how small, towards a particular goal.
For example:

  • Do one push-up
  • Read 5 pages of a book
  • Etc,...

While the activities given as an example above aren't huge, any step taken towards achieving your goals is good and better than zero.
When you find a way to have consistent non-zero days, you will find your motivation increases as you go.
